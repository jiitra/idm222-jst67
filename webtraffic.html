Brand Identity & Concept

Web Traffic is an original game concept for mobile and browser enjoyment. It is a casual free-to-play endless runner game where users compete for high scores on the leaderboard. The user plays as a car, and the goal is to avoid obstacles for as long as possible. But, Web Traffic has a twist. There are three types of levels: high, medium, and low traffic. In high traffic levels, other cars are the main obstacles. In low traffic levels, the environment is the main obstacle. Before playing a level, the user can input a website URL or select popular sites from a list. Web Traffic selects a level based on how popular the inputted website is. Essentially, the level style is based on the inputted website’s popularity, or traffic.

The target audience for Web Traffic is women who are casual mobile game enjoyers. According to a 2017 report from Google Play, 64% of women prefer mobile gaming over other platforms, as opposed to only 38% of men. Since women encompass over half of the active market, this demographic should be recognized. Furthermore, Web Traffic is easily accessible because it is free-to-play and fast-paced.

Visual Design

My color palette was inspired by road signage and other colors prominent on the road. I chose an energetic color palette to convey the fast pace of gameplay. Similarly, I chose Variex as a decorative typeface to maintain the energy and create movement in the lettering. I chose Filson soft as the body text to contrast the sharpness of Variex and bring something more contemporary into the design.

My interactive prototype is a website explaining Web Traffic, featuring the chosen color palette and font pairings. I prioritized strong visual hierarchy and legibility in my final design.
