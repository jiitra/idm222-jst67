Spike's Underwater Adventure

2019 | app development

graphic design lead

overview

Spike’s Underwater Adventure is a mobile game for children ages 4-7. The levels include activities like coloring, tapping, and sorting items. The app aims to improve children’s motor skills in engaging ways.

As the graphic design lead, I designed Spike the Pufferfish and other graphics for the game. I created the intro animation using Adobe After Effects with graphics created in Adobe Illustrator. On the development side, I coded buttons on pages and linked pages together. The team and I programmed using Java, PHP and MySQL.

My favorite part of the project was testing the app with students from a local elementary school. Students in kindergarten, first grade, and second grade tested the app, with the best results coming from students in kindergarten and first grade. During testing, my team was able to assess how challenging the levels were for students to complete and how connected they felt to the gameplay.

After testing, we determined that the app is best suited for students in kindergarten and first grade. The games were the right balance of challenging and educational for students in this range, and we noticed that the activities were less challenging for the second graders.
